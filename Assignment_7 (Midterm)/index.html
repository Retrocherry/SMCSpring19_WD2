    Welcome to Jeff's

    Since 1999, Jeff uses only the finest and freshest meats and spices to create his famous sausages, hotdogs, burgers, and deli products.

    Whether you are eating your meal in our restaurant, having Jeff’s cater your next event, or bringing home our fine provisions to enjoy later, you are eating delicious food made fresh every day, not shipped across the country. We guarantee it!

    Jeff's at Dodger Stadium

    Since operating a temporary cart at Dodgers Jewish Community Nights in 2013, fans can now enjoy Jeff's at all home games except for those that fall on Fridays, Saturdays, and Jewish holidays.

    Find our stand in the right field plaza next to Tommy Lasorda’s Italian Trattoria. Find out more about Jeff's bringing kosher dogs to Dodgers Stadium at the Jewish Journal and CBS2 News.
