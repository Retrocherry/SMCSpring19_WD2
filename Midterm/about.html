    Visit us on Pico!

    All sausages, hotdogs, burgers, and deli meats are made on-site in our factory. Ever since hearing stories about his "Zayde" Isaac’s homemade pastrami, corned beef, and cherry vishnik, Jeff had dreamed of crafting his own unique delicatessen. Savor the flavor of classic age-old traditional recipes and experience the richness of nouveau sausages fashioned with modern sensibilities. Relish the joy of finely cured and cooked meats and the thrill of creative salads and slaws. At Jeff’s Gourmet, we make everything from scratch, using the finest meats, fresh herbs, stocks, and California wines.
